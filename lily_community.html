Nana

1.This document, formulated in accordance with the Nana Community Convention, is made to strengthen management and regulation on short video content and provide Nana users with a healthy and harmonious platform for displaying and appreciating talents.

2. Definition of in-compliant content: all short video content and comments should be strictly in compliance with provisions of the Nana Community Convention, and certain rules shall be referred to as appropriate. Please see the Nana Community Convention for further information.

3. Videos containing the following contents are strictly prohibited from being uploaded:

3.1 Any speech against the country, the government, irreligious speech, or acts which insult or slander the government, the country or religions, for example: Endangering the national security, divulging national secrecy, threatening to overturn state power, or undermining national unity. Inciting national hatred, ethnic discrimination, or undermining national unity. Spreading violence, terrorism or abet crimes. Inciting illegal assembly or formation gathering to disturb social order. Spreading or inciting religious or ethnic discrimination. Undermining national religious policy or advocating heresy and feudalistic superstition.

3.2 Videos containing contents of sexual behavior performance or pornographic content are prohibited from spreading and playing.

3.3 It is prohibited to spread or play gambling-related content. Behaviors such as broadcasting gambling or inciting others to gamble are not allowed.

3.4 It is prohibited to expose others privacy, pose threat to others safety, imperil public interests, injure or kill others, mistreat animals, do bloody or violent behaviors, demonstrate guns or dress up like a terrorist.

3.5 Spreading drugs is prohibited. It is not allowed to demonstrate drug samples, perform drug taking, spread drug use or injection methods, or introduce ways to make drugs.

3.6 It is prohibited to damage the interest of the official platform, including behaviors like promoting competitive products, viciously soliciting and publishing fake ads.

4.The following contents should better not be uploaded, including but not limited to:

4.1 Short videos with a pure-colored cover or dim cover.

4.2 Videos that are too short (no less than 5 seconds).

4.3 Videos without any quality contents, such as static pictures, vague pictures, dim light or randomly taken videos.

5 Definition of in-compliant themes and comments, including but not limited to:

5.1 Inciting behaviors that may disturb social order, including regional assaulting, religious abuse and personal attacks.

5.2 Any political topics that may cause or have caused bad influence.

6.Classification of punishments Users conducting the behaviors above shall be prohibited from using certain functions and interacting with others. As to users with severe violations or breaking the rules repeatedly, their accounts might be frozen and they will no longer be able to use Nana.

7. Nana reserves the final right of interpretation of this document, and Nana has the right to change and publish this document. 8 This document shall come into force as of the date of promulgation.
